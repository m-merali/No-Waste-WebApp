RECYCLABLE ITEMS

PAPER INFO

  • * These can be easily recycled in most places. However, a mistake often made
    is that people add items to recycling that are not recyclable.
    Common mistakes involve leaving food residue on their cardboards.
  • * Paper requires more energy to produce than plastic. About 3 times as much. In addition, it is also generally heavier
    to transport than plastic resulting in high vehicle emissions.
  • * However recycled paper is far more energy efficient using 70% less when made than raw materials.
