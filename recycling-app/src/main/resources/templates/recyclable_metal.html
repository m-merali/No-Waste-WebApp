ALUMINIUM INFO

Plastics and Aluminium are often recycled together. Make sure they are thoroughly cleaned and placed on the right recycling bins.

Infinitely and easily recyclable. Most abundant element, and metal on the earths crust. As a result, it is one of the most widely used and common materials, making up cars, bikes, drink cans etc. Aluminium production requires an immense amount of energy, suing large amounts if water and causes soil pollution. Transportation also leaves a notable carbon footprint. However, recycling aluminium would decrease the need for making more. Statistics say about 54% of Americans recycle their cans.
